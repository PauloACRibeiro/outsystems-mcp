# Code of Conduct

This project follows the **OutSystems Community Code of Conduct**. Everyone interacting in this repository — issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other project space — is expected to honor it.

The full text lives at:

<https://www.outsystems.com/forums/discussion/27050/outsystems-community-code-of-conduct/>

## In short

We expect everyone to:

- **Be considerate** of how your posts and contributions affect other members.
- **Be respectful** and diplomatic in disagreements; no personal attacks.
- **Be responsible** for your words and actions; own and correct mistakes.
- **Be collaborative**; work together to solve shared problems.
- **Be yourself** — use your real name or a real-life nickname.
- **Ask for help** with precise, well-scoped questions when you need it, and **be helpful** when someone else does.
- **Watch your language**; no profanity, threats, or pornographic content.
- **Be careful with links**; only share links you've verified are safe.

The following are not tolerated:

- Spam, pyramid schemes, affiliate marketing, or unsolicited commercial advertising.
- Personal attacks or threats.
- Profane, obscene, or pornographic content.
- Claiming someone else's work as your own without citation.
- Pirated OutSystems products, or links to pirated software.
- Malicious links or malware.

## Reporting violations

If you witness or experience a violation in this repository, contact the OutSystems Community team at **communityteam@outsystems.com**. You can also reach out to the maintainers privately via a GitHub security advisory on this repo.

The community team handles community-wide enforcement; repository maintainers handle scoped action within this repo (locking threads, removing comments, blocking abusive accounts).
